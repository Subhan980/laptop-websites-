Education system

The education system encompasses all institutions involved in formal learning, including schools, universities, and their associated resources and rules. It plays a crucial role in personal development, societal progress, and economic growth by imparting knowledge, skills, and values. A well-functioning education system fosters critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and social equality.
